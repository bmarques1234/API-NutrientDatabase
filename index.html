Help

On the top of the page is located the navigation bar. This bar provide both navigation (left side) on the site and search (right side) for food details.

The first item (left to right) in the right side have 2 options. The first specifies that the search is for food and the second that the search is for nutrients.

The second item have some filters to the search, like the name of the food, its ID, its food group, and the nutrient ID.

If you search for a specified ID the result will be the nutrient values of the food, and if you search for a name the result will be a list of foods with that name (or part of it).

Likewise, if you search for a nutrient ID the result will be the foods with that nutrient.

About

USDA

USDA (United States Department of Agriculture) provide leadership on food, agriculture, natural resources, rural development, nutrition, and related issues based on public policy, the best available science, and effective management.

They have a vision to provide economic opportunity through innovation, helping rural America to thrive; to promote agriculture production that better nourishes Americans while also helping feed others throughout the world; and to preserve their Nation's natural resources through conservation, restored forests, improved watersheds, and healthy private working lands. And their strategic plan serves as a roadmap for the Department to help ensure they achieve their mission and implement their vision.

The Agricultural Research Service (ARS) is the U.S. Department of Agriculture's chief scientific in-house research agency. Their job is finding solutions to agricultural problems that affect Americans every day from field to table. And they provide the USDA National Nutrient Database for Standard Reference and the USDA Branded Food Products Database (which API is used in this site).

Nutritudy

This site use the USDA National Nutrient Database to bring to the user detailed information about all types of food, like the nutritional info of the food sought. For more info about how to search click in the help button.
